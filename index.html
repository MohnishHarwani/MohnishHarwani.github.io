About

I'm a graduating senior at Purdue University majoring in Computer Science. My resaerch focuses on developing better training algorithms for large neural neural networks, and leveraging AI to accelerate scientific research.


Recently, I've been working with nanoHUB to develop agents that perform autonomous research leveraging software tools, public data repositories, and HPC clusters. We expect our Autonomous Universal Research Assistant (AURA) to be available to the public soon. I've also been exploring meta-learned neural network optimizers that can extract more generalizable and otherwise useful information during backpropogation. Feel free to email me if you'd like to connect.
